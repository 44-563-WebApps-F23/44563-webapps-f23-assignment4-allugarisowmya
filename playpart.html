Title:The master Builder
Author:henrik ibsen
BROVIK

[Impatiently.]

Go to bed, I suppose? Would you have me stifled outright?

KAIA

Then take a little walk.

RAGNAR

Yes, do. I will come with you.

BROVIK

[With warmth.]

I will not go till he comes! I and determined to have it out this evening withhim the chief.

KAIA

[Anxiously.]

Oh no, uncle,--do wait awhile before doing that!

RAGNAR

Yes, better wait, father!

BROVIK

[Draws is breath laboriously.]

Ha--ha--! _I_ haven't much time for waiting.
